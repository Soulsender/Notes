### OSPF
- LSUs contain different link-state advertisements
- hello > DBD > LSR > LSU > LSA
- down > init >two-way > exstart > exchange > loading > full
- lower priority, highest RID
### ACLs
- routers can have 4 acls on one interface
	- in/out IPv4
	- in/out IPv6
### NAT
